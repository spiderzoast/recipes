Chicken Yakisoba

Description

This traditional Japanese yakisoba noodle dish includes cabbage and chicken in a spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Asian-style chile paste
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • ½ medium head cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 carrots, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 pounds cooked yakisoba noodles
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger, or to taste (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons canola oil and sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir chicken and garlic in hot oil until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir chile paste into chicken mixture.
  4. Cook and stir until chicken is completely browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce and simmer for 2 minutes. Pour chicken and sauce into a bowl.
  6. Heat 1 tablespoon canola oil in the skillet over medium-high heat.
  7. Cook and stir cabbage, onion, carrots, and salt in hot oil until cabbage is wilted, 3 to 4 minutes.
  8. Stir the chicken mixture into the cabbage mixture.
  9. Add noodles.
  10. Cook and stir until noodles are hot and chicken is no longer pink inside, 3 to 4 minutes.
  11. Garnish with pickled ginger.
